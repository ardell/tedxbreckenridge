{%- comment -%} Crop marks include — emits 4 L-shaped corner marks at the trim line of a printed piece. Required params (all in inches): - trim_w: trim width - trim_h: trim height - bleed: bleed amount on each side (typically 0.125) - slug: additional space outside the bleed where the marks live (typically 0.25) The parent container must be `position: relative` and sized to (trim_w + 2*bleed + 2*slug) × (trim_h + 2*bleed + 2*slug). The artwork (trim + bleed) is centered inside via a `slug` inset; this include positions marks at the four trim corners. Mark geometry: - Gap from trim edge to start of mark line: 0.0625in - Mark length: 0.1875in - Stroke: 0.5pt black, no anti-aliased softening {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign trim_w = include.trim_w -%} {%- assign trim_h = include.trim_h -%} {%- assign bleed = include.bleed -%} {%- assign slug = include.slug -%} {%- comment -%} Trim corners (in inches from the outer page edge) {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign trim_left = slug | plus: bleed -%} {%- assign trim_top = slug | plus: bleed -%} {%- assign trim_right_from_edge = slug | plus: bleed -%} {%- assign trim_bottom_from_edge = slug | plus: bleed -%}
