and Art of Cooking at Altitude

Bernhard Schrag

Associate Professor of Culinary Arts, Colorado Mountain College

Bernhard Schrag teaches culinary arts at CMC's Vail Valley campus in Edwards. With degrees in adult and vocational education from Australia and New Zealand, he brings a global perspective to high-altitude cooking. Beyond the classroom, Bernhard is an avid outdoorsman who enjoys camping with family, foraging for porcini mushrooms, and instructing skiing at Keystone.
