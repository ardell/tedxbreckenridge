## Repurposing Rules

-   Every email feature becomes a blog post.
-   Every blog post becomes 2--3 social snippets.
-   Speaker features repeat again during event buildup.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Volunteer Workflow (Monthly)

1.  Confirm the month's theme and events.
2.  Draft one email + 1--2 blog posts.
3.  Repurpose snippets for social.
4.  Schedule email.
5.  Link email to any deeper blog content.
6.  Record metrics.
7.  Adjust next month.
