We're thrilled to announce that TEDxBreckenridge is returning this fall with an all-new theme: Kaleidoscope.

Just like a kaleidoscope transforms simple fragments into something extraordinary, this year's event will bring together diverse perspectives, ideas, and voices — each one shifting the way we see the world around us. Expect inspiring talks, meaningful conversations, and a community of curious, forward-thinking people ready to explore ideas worth spreading.

Mark your calendar — ticketing details and speaker announcements are coming soon.

Join Our Volunteer Team

TEDxBreckenridge is organized entirely by local volunteers — and we're growing our team for Kaleidoscope. Whether you want to help with event planning, marketing, speaker coordination, or day-of logistics, there's a place for you.

No experience required — just curiosity and a willingness to be part of something meaningful in our community.
