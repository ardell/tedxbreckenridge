This year's theme

Turn it upside down.
See what falls out.

Inversion is a way of thinking. Instead of asking "How do I succeed?", ask "How would I guarantee failure?" Instead of following the path forward, trace it backward. The answers often surprise us.

This year's speakers will explore inversions across science, art, community, and identity. They'll share moments when flipping the question revealed something essential — and invite you to do the same.

Sometimes the clearest view comes from standing on your head.
