## What is TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event.

## About TEDxBreckenridge

TEDxBreckenridge brings the spirit of TED to the beautiful mountain community of Breckenridge, Colorado. Our event features local voices, innovative thinkers, and inspiring stories that reflect both our mountain culture and global perspectives.

### Our Mission

To create a platform for innovative ideas and meaningful conversations that inspire positive change in our community and beyond.

### Our Values

- **Innovation**: Celebrating creative thinking and bold ideas
- **Community**: Building connections across diverse perspectives
- **Authenticity**: Honoring genuine voices and experiences
- **Sustainability**: Promoting responsible stewardship of our environment

## The TED License

This independent TEDx event is operated under license from TED. TEDx events are organized by passionate individuals who seek to uncover new ideas and to share the latest research in their local areas that spark conversations in their communities.
