# TEDxBreckenridge Brand Style Guide

**Ideas Worth Spreading in the High Rockies**

---

## Mission

To give voice to expansive ideas and disruptive concepts that shift the way we see, connect, and evolve within our mountain community.

## Core Values

**IDEAS** — We believe that collective genius and expansive ideas can improve our community and ultimately, the world.

**COMMUNITY** — We serve and support a local experience unique to our mountain communities that is inclusive, connection-centered, and culturally beneficial.

**AUTHENTICITY** — We are committed in heart and mind to genuine, passionate, and authentic experiences and connections.

## Brand Voice & Tone

| We Are | Not |
|--------|-----|
| Inspirational | Cliché |
| Genuine | Pretentious |
| Down-to-Earth | Pompous |
| Curious | Rigid |
| Imaginative | Closed-Minded |
| Inquisitive | Self-Righteous |
| Collaborative | Divisive |
| Conversational | Scripted |
| Playful | Strict |
| Inclusive | Exclusive |
