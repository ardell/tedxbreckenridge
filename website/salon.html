TEDxBreckenridge Salon

Small Gatherings, Big Ideas

TEDxBreckenridge Salon events are intimate, conversational gatherings that bring together curious minds to explore fascinating topics. Unlike our main annual event, Salons are smaller by design—creating space for deeper discussion, questions, and genuine connection.

Each Salon features a short talk or presentation from a local expert, followed by guided conversation where everyone's voice matters. It's learning that feels less like a lecture and more like sitting around a table with friends who love to explore ideas together.

Topics vary throughout the year—from science and technology to art, culture, and community challenges. What stays constant is the spirit: curiosity-driven, accessible to all, and designed to leave you thinking differently about the world around you.
