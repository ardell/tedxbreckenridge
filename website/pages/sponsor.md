<h2>Why Partner with TEDxBreckenridge</h2>

<p>The successful production of TEDxBreckenridge requires the financial support of local businesses and organizations. Your sponsorship will expand innovative thinking and collaborative connections throughout our local community, while reaching an engaged audience of thought-leaders.</p>

<p>With your help, we will bring Summit County together in a day of thoughtful conversation on ideas worth sharing. Our 2026 event will create an opportunity for speakers, participants, and volunteers to explore the ways we connect to our community, to our mountain environment, to each other, and to ourselves.</p>

<p>Thank you for your interest in partnering with TEDxBreckenridge. Our sponsorship packages have been designed to offer flexibility and meet the unique promotional needs of your organization. In-kind contributions for needed materials and services will also be accepted as forms of sponsorship support.</p>
