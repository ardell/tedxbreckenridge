faqs:
  - q: When and where is the next TEDxBreckenridge event?
    a: >-
      Our main event takes place annually each October at the Riverwalk Center in Breckenridge, Colorado. The next event is scheduled for October 3, 2026. Throughout the year, we also host intimate Salon events at various locations around Summit County. Check our [homepage](/) for the latest event dates and [join our mailing list](/#mailing-list) to stay updated.
  - q: How do I get tickets?
    a: >-
      Tickets for our main event typically go on sale several months before the event date. We offer various ticket tiers to make the event accessible to our community. For our Salon events, we use a suggested donation model—ideas should be available to everyone, regardless of ability to pay. [Join our mailing list](/#mailing-list) to be notified when tickets become available.
  - q: Are there group tickets available?
    a: >-
      Yes! If you have a group of 10 or more people interested in attending together, we can often arrange special group pricing. Please reach out to us at [ideas@tedxbreckenridge.com](mailto:ideas@tedxbreckenridge.com) to discuss group ticket options.
  - q: Is the event child-friendly?
    a: >-
      Yes! Our events are family-friendly and welcome attendees of all ages. We believe that inspiring ideas can resonate with people at any stage of life. Parents should use their judgment about whether the content and format will engage their children—talks typically last 10-18 minutes each and cover a wide range of topics.
  - q: What's the dress code?
    a: >-
      Mountain Casual—wear whatever makes you comfortable! This is a relaxed, welcoming environment where you'll see everything from hiking boots to business casual. We want you to focus on the ideas, not your outfit. That said, many attendees enjoy dressing up a bit for the occasion since it's a special evening out.
  - q: Can I volunteer for the event?
    a: >-
      Absolutely! TEDxBreckenridge is run entirely by passionate local volunteers, and we'd love to have you join our team. Whether you want to help on event day or contribute your skills year-round to our planning committee, there's a place for you. Visit our [volunteer page](/volunteer/) to learn more about opportunities and fill out the volunteer interest form.
  - q: How can I apply to speak at TEDxBreckenridge?
    a: >-
      We're always looking for compelling local voices with ideas worth spreading! Speaker applications are typically open year-round, and we review submissions for our upcoming events. The selection process includes written applications, live auditions, and speaker coaching. Learn more about the process and submit your idea on our [speaker application page](/speak/).
  - q: What should I bring to the event?
    a: >-
      Bring yourself, an open mind, and curiosity! Many attendees like to bring a notebook and pen to jot down insights and ideas that resonate with them. We're bringing together the brightest minds in Summit County to explore new ways of thinking about the issues that matter to our community—you'll want to capture those moments of inspiration.
  - q: Will the talks be available online after the event?
    a: >-
      Yes! All main event talks are professionally recorded and published to our [YouTube channel](https://youtube.com/@tedxbreckenridge){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} in the weeks following the event. You can also watch [all previous talks](/talks/) from our five years of events. This allows our local ideas to reach audiences around the world.
  - q: Is there parking available at the venue?
    a: >-
      Yes, there is public parking available near the Riverwalk Center in downtown Breckenridge. We encourage carpooling when possible, and the venue is also accessible via the free Summit Stage bus system. Detailed parking and transportation information is provided to ticket holders closer to the event date.
  - q: How can my business become a sponsor?
    a: >-
      We partner with local businesses and organizations who share our passion for bringing ideas worth spreading to Summit County. Sponsorship provides excellent visibility while supporting a meaningful community cause. Visit our [sponsorship page](/sponsor/) to learn about partnership opportunities and benefits, or contact our sponsorship team directly.
  - q: I have more questions about TEDxBreckenridge. How can I contact you?
    a: >-
      We'd love to hear from you! [Visit our contact page](/contact/) to fill out our contact form, and a real human from our team will get back to you as soon as possible. You can also reach us directly at [ideas@tedxbreckenridge.com](mailto:ideas@tedxbreckenridge.com).
