## Introduction

TEDxBreckenridge is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing ideas worth spreading to Summit County, Colorado. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and being transparent about the data we collect.

This privacy policy applies to tedxbreckenridge.com and describes how we collect, use, and protect information about our visitors.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. **Google Analytics automatically collects:**

- **Page views** - Which pages you visit and how long you spend on them
- **Device information** - Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **General location** - City and region (not precise location)
- **Referral source** - How you found our website (search engine, social media, etc.)
- **Browsing behavior** - Navigation patterns and interactions with our site

**Google Analytics does NOT collect:**
- Personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number
- Financial information
- Precise geolocation data

### Voluntarily Provided Information

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us:

- **Name and email address** - When you sign up for our mailing list
- **Contact information** - When you submit inquiries through contact forms
- **Payment information** - When you purchase tickets or make donations (processed securely by Ticketsauce and Givebutter; we do not store payment details)

### How We Share Your Information

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Track you across other websites

**We DO share information with trusted service providers:**
- **Mailchimp** - Email addresses for subscribers who opt in to our mailing list
- **Ticketsauce** - Contact information when you purchase event tickets
- **Givebutter** - Contact information when you make donations
- **Google Analytics** - Anonymized browsing data (no personal identifiers)

These service providers use your information only to provide services on our behalf and are obligated to protect your data.

## Why We Collect It

We collect website usage data to:

- **Understand how visitors use our site** - Which pages are most popular, where visitors spend time
- **Improve user experience** - Identify issues, optimize navigation, and make the site easier to use
- **Measure event interest and reach** - Track interest in our events and speakers
- **Make informed decisions about content** - Create content that resonates with our community

All data collection is focused on improving the experience for our local Summit County community and visitors interested in attending TEDxBreckenridge events.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

### Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google may use this data according to their own privacy policy. For more information:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

When you purchase event tickets through our website, you are redirected to Ticketsauce's platform. Ticketsauce handles all payment processing and ticket fulfillment. See their privacy policy:
- [Ticketsauce Privacy Policy](https://www.ticketsauce.com/privacy-policy)

### Givebutter (Donations)
When you make a donation through our website, you are redirected to Givebutter's platform. Givebutter handles all payment processing. See their privacy policy:
- [Givebutter Privacy Policy](https://givebutter.com/privacy-policy)

## Your Rights & How to Opt-Out

You have control over your data and can opt-out of our services at any time.

### Unsubscribe from Mailing List
Every email we send includes an "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom. Click it to be immediately removed from our mailing list. You can also:
- Manage your subscription preferences using the link in any email
- Email us at [info@tedxbreckenridge.com](mailto:info@tedxbreckenridge.com) to request removal

### Opt-Out of Google Analytics
Install the **Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on**:
- [Download the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

This browser extension prevents Google Analytics from tracking your activity across all websites that use Google Analytics.

### Manage Browser Cookies
You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect your experience on some websites.

### Contact Us About Your Data
If you have questions about what data we have or would like to request information, please contact us at [info@tedxbreckenridge.com](mailto:info@tedxbreckenridge.com).

## Cookies We Use

Our website uses the following cookies:

### Google Analytics Cookies

- **`_ga`** - Distinguishes unique visitors (expires after 2 years)
- **`_gid`** - Distinguishes unique visitors (expires after 24 hours)
- **`_gat`** - Used to throttle request rate (expires after 1 minute)

These cookies are used solely for analytics purposes and do not contain personally identifiable information.

### No Advertising or Tracking Cookies
We do NOT use:
- Advertising cookies
- Social media tracking pixels (beyond basic share buttons)
- Third-party tracking or retargeting cookies

## Data Retention

### Google Analytics
We retain Google Analytics data for **26 months** (the default Google Analytics 4 setting). After 26 months, data is automatically deleted from Google's servers.

### Mailing List
Email addresses remain in our Mailchimp account until you unsubscribe. Once you unsubscribe, your email address is removed from our active mailing list but may be retained in Mailchimp's system for compliance purposes (preventing accidental re-subscription).

### Contact Form Submissions
If you contact us via email or contact form, we retain your message as long as needed for communication purposes. You may request deletion of your messages at any time by contacting us.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we collect:

- **SSL/HTTPS Encryption** - All data transmitted to and from our website is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/HTTPS protocols
- **No Payment Processing** - We do not process payments directly on our site; all transactions are handled by trusted third-party services (Ticketsauce, Givebutter)
- **Google's Security** - Analytics data is stored by Google Analytics; see their security practices at [Google Cloud Security](https://cloud.google.com/security)

## Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at [info@tedxbreckenridge.com](mailto:info@tedxbreckenridge.com), and we will promptly delete it.

Our events are family-friendly, but our website and online services are designed for adults.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons.

When we make changes:
- We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
- We encourage you to review this policy periodically
- Significant changes will be noted on our website

Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
